When someone in the meeting gives you access, you’ll join it.

If you don’t have enough time to schedule a meeting or book a room, you can join a video meeting with a link.

  1. Open the chat message or email with the meeting link.
  2. Click the meeting link.
  3. In the window that opens, click Join now.

Tip: If you're not on the calendar invite, you need to request to join and the meeting organizer must let you in.
